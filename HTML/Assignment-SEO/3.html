Why SEO Matters

  1. It helps more people find your website when they search online for things you offer.
  2. This means more visitors, who could become customers or clients.
  3. It makes your site easier to use, so people stay longer and enjoy it more.
  4. Your website looks more professional and trustworthy when it's in top search results.
  5. It's a cheaper way to get visitors compared to paying for ads.
  6. You can beat your competitors by showing up higher in search results.
